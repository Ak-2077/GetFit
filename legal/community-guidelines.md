# Community Guidelines — AI Trainer Chatbot

**Last Updated:** June 26, 2026

These Community Guidelines apply to your interactions with the **AI Trainer chatbot** within the GetFit application operated by [COMPANY_NAME].

---

## 1. Purpose of the AI Trainer

The AI Trainer is designed to:
- Provide personalized fitness and nutrition coaching
- Answer questions about workouts, diet, exercises, and healthy living
- Help you track progress and stay motivated
- Offer adaptive recommendations based on your profile and goals

---

## 2. Acceptable Use

When interacting with the AI Trainer, you should:

- ✅ Ask genuine fitness, nutrition, and health-related questions
- ✅ Provide accurate information about your health, injuries, and limitations
- ✅ Use the AI as a supplementary tool alongside professional guidance
- ✅ Report any AI responses that seem harmful, inaccurate, or inappropriate
- ✅ Treat the AI Trainer as a tool, not as a substitute for a qualified professional

---

## 3. Prohibited Conduct

You must NOT use the AI Trainer to:

- ❌ **Seek emergency medical advice** — If you are experiencing a medical emergency, call your local emergency services immediately
- ❌ **Request harmful content** — Including content promoting self-harm, eating disorders, dangerous weight loss methods, or substance abuse
- ❌ **Generate abusive or discriminatory content** — Including hate speech, harassment, or content targeting any individual or group
- ❌ **Exploit the AI system** — Including attempts to extract system prompts, manipulate the AI into violating its guidelines (jailbreaking), or use the AI for purposes outside fitness and nutrition
- ❌ **Share illegal content** — Including copyrighted material, illegal substances, or content that violates any applicable law
- ❌ **Provide false health information** — Deliberately providing inaccurate health data (e.g., fake allergies) to receive inappropriate recommendations
- ❌ **Use the AI for minors** — The App is intended for users aged 16+. Do not use the AI to generate plans for children under 16

---

## 4. AI Trainer Limitations

Please understand that the AI Trainer:

- Is **not a licensed healthcare professional**, dietitian, or certified personal trainer
- May provide **inaccurate information** — always verify important health decisions with a professional
- Cannot diagnose or treat medical conditions
- Cannot prescribe medications or supplements
- May not account for all individual health factors, medication interactions, or medical conditions
- Does not provide mental health counseling or therapy

---
