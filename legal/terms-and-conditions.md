## 3. Description of Service

GetFit is a fitness and nutrition tracking application that provides:

- **Fitness tracking** — Step counting, active calorie burn tracking, and distance measurement via Apple HealthKit (iOS) and Health Connect (Android)
- **Calorie and nutrition tracking** — Manual food logging, barcode scanning, AI-powered food recognition from camera images, and macro/calorie tracking
- **BMI Calculator** — Body Mass Index calculation based on height and weight
- **Balance Meal Meter (BMB)** — Macro balance analysis of meals
- **Workout modules** — Home workouts, gym workouts, body-part-specific exercises, and workout plans
- **Diet plans** — Pre-built and AI-generated personalized diet plans
- **AI Trainer chatbot** — Personalized fitness and nutrition coaching powered by artificial intelligence
- **AI Video Analysis** — AI-powered exercise form analysis from user-submitted videos
- **Nutrition streaks** — Daily nutrition tracking with gamification
- **Subscription management** — Free, Kyro Pro, and Kyro Pro+ tier subscriptions
- **Notifications** — In-app fitness progress notifications
- **Search** — Global search across workouts, exercises, and foods
