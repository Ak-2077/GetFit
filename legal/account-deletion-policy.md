# Account Deletion Policy

**Last Updated:** June 26, 2026

This Account Deletion Policy explains how you can delete your **GetFit** account and what happens to your data when you do.

---

## 1. How to Delete Your Account

### 1.1 In-App Deletion (Recommended)
1. Open the GetFit app
2. Navigate to **Profile** tab
3. Tap **Settings** (gear icon)
4. Scroll to the bottom of the settings page
5. Tap **Delete Account**
6. Confirm the deletion when prompted

### 1.2 Via Email
If you are unable to access the App, you can request account deletion by emailing [CONTACT_EMAIL] with:
- The email address or phone number associated with your account
- A statement requesting account deletion

We will process email deletion requests within **30 days**.

---

When you delete your account, the following data is **permanently and irreversibly deleted**:

| Data Category | Deleted |

| Account profile (name, email, phone, avatar) | ✅ Permanently deleted |
| Authentication data (password hash, Google/Apple IDs) | ✅ Permanently deleted |

| Fitness targets (calorie goals, protein targets, move targets) | ✅ Permanently deleted |
| Food logs (all meal entries) | ✅ Permanently deleted |
| Burn logs (exercise calorie burns) | ✅ Permanently deleted |
| Nutrition streaks | ✅ Permanently deleted |
| Food memory (frequent/recent foods) | ✅ Permanently deleted |
| AI chat sessions (conversation history) | ✅ Permanently deleted |
| AI memories (all extracted facts) | ✅ Permanently deleted |
| AI user state (energy, fatigue, motivation, etc.) | ✅ Permanently deleted |
| AI learning profile | ✅ Permanently deleted |
| Digital twin data | ✅ Permanently deleted |
| Video analysis records | ✅ Permanently deleted |
| Notifications | ✅ Permanently deleted |
| OTP records | ✅ Permanently deleted |

---

## 3. What Is Retained After Deletion

| Data Category | Retention Period | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| Subscription and payment records | 7 years | Required by Indian tax and financial regulations |
| Anonymous, aggregated analytics | Indefinitely | Cannot be linked back to your identity |
| Data in encrypted database backups | Up to 30 days | Standard backup rotation before permanent purge |

---

## 4. Important Considerations

### 4.1 Active Subscriptions
- Deleting your account does **not** automatically cancel active subscriptions
- **iOS:** You must cancel your subscription through Apple Settings → Subscriptions before deleting your account
- **Android:** Active Razorpay subscriptions will not renew after account deletion, but any remaining subscription period will be forfeited
- **No refunds** are issued for unused subscription periods after account deletion

### 4.2 Irreversibility
- Account deletion is **permanent and irreversible**
- We cannot recover your data after deletion is processed
- If you wish to use GetFit again, you will need to create a new account

### 4.3 Processing Time
- In-app deletion is processed **immediately**
- Email-requested deletion is processed within **30 days**
- Data may persist in encrypted backups for up to 30 additional days after deletion

### 4.4 Signed-In Sessions
- After account deletion, you will be automatically signed out
- Your authentication token will become invalid

---

## 5. Partial Data Deletion

You can delete specific data without deleting your entire account:

| What to Delete | How |
|---|---|
| Individual AI memories | AI Trainer → Memories → Delete specific memory |
| All AI memories | AI Trainer → Memories → Reset All |
| Individual chat sessions | AI Trainer → Chat History → Delete session |
| Individual food log entries | Calories tab → Swipe or tap to delete entry |
| Individual burn log entries | Calories tab → Swipe or tap to delete entry |
| Profile photo | Settings → Profile → Remove photo |

---

## 6. Apple App Store Compliance

This account deletion feature complies with Apple App Store Review Guideline **5.1.1(v)**, which requires apps that support account creation to also allow users to initiate deletion of their account from within the app.

---

## 7. Google Play Compliance

This account deletion feature complies with Google Play's **User Data policy**, which requires apps to provide users with an in-app path to request deletion of their account and associated data.
