## 2. Subscription Purchase

### 2.1 iOS (Apple App Store)
- Subscriptions are processed through **Apple In-App Purchase**
- Payment is charged to your **Apple ID** account
- Prices may vary by region based on Apple's pricing tiers
- Apple's standard in-app purchase terms apply

### 2.2 Android (Google Play)
- Subscriptions are processed through **Razorpay**
- Payment is charged to your selected payment method (UPI, credit/debit card, net banking, etc.)
- Prices are in Indian Rupees (INR)

---

## 3. Subscription Activation

- Your subscription becomes active **immediately** after successful payment verification
- Server-side verification ensures payment integrity before granting access
- You will receive an in-app confirmation of your subscription status

---

## 4. Subscription Duration

- **Monthly subscriptions** are valid for **30 days** from the date of purchase
- **Yearly subscriptions** are valid for **365 days** from the date of purchase
- After the subscription period expires, your account will revert to the **Free** plan
- You will retain access to your data but will lose access to premium features

---

## 5. Subscription Renewal

- GetFit subscriptions are currently **non-auto-renewing**
- You will need to manually purchase a new subscription when your current one expires
- We may introduce auto-renewable subscriptions in the future, and will notify you of any such changes

## 6. Cancellation

### 6.1 iOS Subscriptions
If you purchased through the Apple App Store:
1. Open your device **Settings**
2. Tap your **Apple ID** at the top
3. Tap **Subscriptions**
4. Find **GetFit** and tap **Cancel Subscription**
5. Follow the on-screen instructions

Alternatively, you can manage subscriptions at [https://apps.apple.com/account/subscriptions](https://apps.apple.com/account/subscriptions).

### 6.2 Android Subscriptions
Since Android subscriptions are non-auto-renewing (one-time purchases for a fixed period), no cancellation action is required — the subscription will simply expire at the end of the purchased period.

### 6.3 Effect of Cancellation/Expiry
- You will continue to have access to premium features until the end of your paid subscription period
- After expiry, your account will revert to the Free plan
- Your data (food logs, AI memories, chat history, etc.) will **not** be deleted when your subscription expires or is cancelled
- You can re-subscribe at any time to regain premium access

## 7. Refund Policy

### 7.1 iOS Refunds
- All iOS purchases are handled by **Apple**
- Refund requests must be submitted directly to Apple:
  - Visit [https://reportaproblem.apple.com](https://reportaproblem.apple.com)
  - Sign in with your Apple ID
  - Select the GetFit purchase
  - Request a refund
- KeyZen cannot process refunds for iOS purchases, as Apple controls all payment processing
- Apple's refund policy applies

### 7.2 Android Refunds (Razorpay)
- **Within 48 hours of purchase:** You may request a full refund by contacting us at [CONTACT_EMAIL] with your order details
- **After 48 hours:** Refunds are evaluated on a case-by-case basis at our discretion
- **Refunds are NOT available** for:
  - Subscriptions that have been substantially used (e.g., significant usage of AI features)
  - Subscriptions cancelled after the refund window
  - Users who violated our Terms & Conditions
- Approved refunds will be processed within **7-10 business days** to your original payment method

### 7.3 Technical Issues
If you experience technical issues that prevent you from using paid features, please contact [CONTACT_EMAIL] with:
- Your account email or phone number
- Description of the issue
- Screenshots or error messages (if applicable)

We will either resolve the technical issue or consider a refund on a case-by-case basis.
