## 3. Accuracy of Calculations

The App provides various calculated metrics including:

| Metric | Method | Limitation |
|---|---|---|
| **BMI** | Weight (kg) / Height (m²) | BMI is a screening tool, not a diagnostic measure. It does not account for muscle mass, bone density, body composition, age, sex, or ethnicity. |
| **Calorie targets** | Mifflin-St Jeor equation with activity multiplier | This is an estimate. Actual calorie needs vary based on individual metabolism, medical conditions, and other factors. |
| **Protein targets** | Based on body weight and fitness goal | This is a general guideline and may not be appropriate for individuals with kidney disease or other conditions. |
| **Nutritional data** | USDA FoodData Central, OpenFoodFacts, AI estimation | Nutritional information is approximate. Actual values vary based on preparation methods, portion sizes, brands, and growing conditions. |
| **Step count** | Apple HealthKit / Health Connect / Device pedometer | Step counts are estimates and may vary in accuracy based on device, carrying position, and walking pattern. |
| **Active calories** | HealthKit / Health Connect calculations | Calorie burn estimates from wearables and health APIs are approximations. |

**These calculations are estimates and should not be used as the sole basis for health decisions.**
