## 3. Accuracy and Reliability

### 3.1 No Guarantee of Accuracy
We do **not** guarantee the accuracy, reliability, completeness, or timeliness of any AI-generated content. AI models can produce errors including but not limited to:
- Incorrect nutritional information
- Inappropriate exercise recommendations for your fitness level
- Diet plans that do not fully account for medical conditions or allergies
- Incorrect identification of food items from images
- Missed or incorrect assessment of exercise form

### 3.2 AI Hallucinations
AI models may occasionally "hallucinate" — generating plausible-sounding but factually incorrect information. Always verify important information from reliable sources.

### 3.3 Food Recognition Accuracy
AI food recognition from camera images is inherently imprecise:
- Similar-looking foods may be confused
- Portion sizes are estimated, not measured
- Mixed dishes may not be fully decomposed into individual ingredients
- Nutritional estimates are approximate

### 3.4 Video Analysis Accuracy
AI exercise form analysis:
- May not detect all form errors
- Camera angle, lighting, and video quality affect accuracy
- Is not a substitute for in-person coaching
- Should not be relied upon as the sole source of form feedback
