# Data Collection Policy

**Last Updated:** June 26, 2026

This Data Collection Policy provides a detailed breakdown of all data collected by the **GetFit** mobile application operated by KeyZen.

---

## 1. Categories of Data Collected

### 1.1 Data You Provide

| Category | Specific Data | When Collected | Required |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Identity** | Name | Onboarding | Yes |
| **Contact** | Phone number | Phone OTP registration | Only if chosen |
| **Contact** | Email address | Email registration or profile | Only if chosen |
| **Credentials** | Password (stored as bcrypt hash) | Email/password registration | Only if chosen |
| **Social Identity** | Google account ID, email, name, avatar | Google Sign-In | Only if chosen |
| **Social Identity** | Apple account ID, email, name | Apple Sign-In | Only if chosen |
| **Profile** | Profile photo (base64 or URL) | Photo upload | No |
| **Location** | Country | Onboarding | No |
| **Health Profile** | Height (cm), weight (kg), age, gender | Onboarding | Yes |
| **Fitness Goals** | Goal (lose/gain/maintain), target weight, timeline | Onboarding | Yes |
| **Preferences** | Activity level, diet preference (veg/non-veg), fitness level | Onboarding | Yes |
| **Nutrition Logs** | Food items, quantities, meal type, calories, protein, carbs, fat | Manual food logging | User-initiated |
| **Water Intake** | Liters consumed per day | Streak updates | User-initiated |
| **Chat Messages** | Text messages to AI Trainer | AI chat sessions | User-initiated |
| **Feedback** | Thumbs up/down on AI responses | AI interaction | User-initiated |

| Category | Specific Data | Source | Purpose |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Health Data** | Step count | Apple HealthKit / Health Connect / Pedometer | Fitness tracking |
| **Health Data** | Active energy burned (kcal) | Apple HealthKit / Health Connect | Calorie tracking |

### 1.3 Data Generated by the App

| Category | Specific Data | How Generated |
|---|---|---|
| **Computed Health Metrics** | BMI, body type (ectomorph/mesomorph/endomorph) | Calculated from height and weight |
| **Computed Nutrition Targets** | Maintenance calories, goal calories, daily protein target | Mifflin-St Jeor equation + goal adjustment |
| **Nutrition Streaks** | Daily completion scores, streak qualification | Computed from food logs vs. targets |
| **AI Memories** | Personal facts (injuries, goals, preferences, body stats) | AI extraction from chat conversations |
| **AI User State** | Energy, recovery, fatigue, motivation, adherence, injury risk | AI computation from usage signals |
| **AI Learning Profile** | Communication preferences, response patterns | AI analysis of interactions |
| **Subscription Status** | Plan tier, expiry date, payment status | Payment verification |

### 1.4 Camera and Image Data

| Data | When Collected | Processing | Storage |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Barcode images** | When scanning products | Processed on-device for barcode decoding | Not stored |
| **Food photos** | When using food recognition | Sent as base64 to AI service for identification | Not permanently stored on server |
| **Profile photos** | When uploading profile picture | Stored as base64 or URL in user profile | Stored until account deletion |

## 2. Data We Do NOT Collect

- ❌ Location data (GPS coordinates) — Location permission is declared for step estimation but we do not track or store your location
- ❌ Audio recordings — Although the camera permission includes microphone access, we do not record or store audio
- ❌ Contacts or address book
- ❌ Browsing history
- ❌ Text messages or call logs
- ❌ Financial information (credit card numbers, bank details) — Payment processing is handled entirely by Razorpay and Apple
- ❌ Biometric data (fingerprint, face ID) — Authentication biometrics are handled by the operating system
- ❌ Precise geolocation tracking

## 4. Permissions and Their Justification

| Permission | Platform | Why Required |
|---|---|---|
| **Camera** | iOS & Android | Barcode scanning for packaged food products; food photo recognition for AI-powered nutritional analysis |
| **HealthKit (Read)** | iOS | Reading step count and active energy burned to display accurate fitness data without manual entry |
| **Health Connect (Read)** | Android | Reading step count, active calories, distance, and exercise data for fitness tracking |
| **Activity Recognition** | Android | Detecting physical activity for accurate step counting via the device pedometer |
| **Internet** | Both | All API communication with our backend for authentication, data sync, AI features, and payment processing |
| **Photo Library** | Both | Selecting profile photos from your gallery |

---

## 5. Data Minimization

We follow the principle of data minimization:
- We only collect data that is necessary for the App's functionality
- Health data from HealthKit/Health Connect is read-only — we do not write health data
- Food images are processed in real-time and not permanently stored
- OTP codes automatically expire after 5 minutes
- AI session memories (Level 4) are destroyed when the chat session ends
